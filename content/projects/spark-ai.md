As AI Foundation Lead at [Readdle](https://readdle.com/), I lead the team responsible for all AI-powered features in [Spark Mail](https://sparkmailapp.com/). We shipped the **AI Assistant** with a fully local RAG pipeline running on all four major platforms - iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows. Building on-device retrieval-augmented generation across such different hardware and OS constraints was a major technical undertaking.

I also led an agent architecture overhaul for rapid model switching. We're continuing to explore new ways AI can make email smarter and more efficient.
