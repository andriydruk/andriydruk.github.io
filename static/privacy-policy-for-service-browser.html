Privacy Policy for Service Browser

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Welcome to Service Browser! I am committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of all users of my app. In this Privacy Policy, I'm providing detailed information on when and why I don't collect personal data, how I use the only permission required (Internet permission), and how I ensure your privacy is protected.

1. No Personal Information Collection

I, Andriy Druk, the developer of Service Browser, do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information from the users of my app. My app is designed with your privacy as a priority, ensuring you can use it without compromising your personal data.

2. Non-Personal Information

Service Browser is designed in such a way that it does not collect or use any non-personal information from its users. The app operates independently without tracking or analyzing user data, behavior, or preferences.

3. Internet Permission Usage

The only permission required by Service Browser is for Internet access. This permission is necessary to discover and browse mDNS/Bonjour services on your local network. All discovered service information (such as service names, types, hostnames, and ports) stays on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

4. Local Data Storage

Service Browser stores your favorite services and registered services locally on your device. This data is never sent to any external servers or shared with third parties.
